What is MyApps4School?

MyApps4School is an ecosystem of apps for schools that I created to bolster confidence and engagement of students, make classrooms more interactive and fun and create a sense of awareness in parents and students to protect mother earth.

ExSell

Exsell is an Ecommerce website for schools. Teachers can motivate students to be more engaged in class, perform well in tests and award class cash. Students can use this class cash to buy items that are posted by their teacher or other students in the class.

ExRideshare

Exrideshare is a carpooling app for schools. It allows parents to join their childrens school and create and join carpools!

ExLearn

Exlearn is an app that allows students & teachers to upload educational content. It is a place where students can learn relevant things from fellow students & teachers. All the contents are verified before showing up on the site.
